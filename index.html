EV Charging Infrastructure

Powering the Future of Mobility, Reliably.

Meeting the EV Demand in South Africa

The Challenge

  • Grid Instability: Frequent load-shedding disrupts charging and creates "charging anxiety" for residents.
  • Overload Risk: Multiple EVs charging simultaneously can overload older building infrastructure, causing trips and failures.
  • Complex Billing: Fairly tracking and billing for shared charger usage is a major administrative hurdle.

The Veralogix Solution

  • Smart Load Management: Our Level 2 AC chargers dynamically balance power, allowing more cars to charge safely on limited capacity.
  • Solar & Battery Integration: We offer seamless integration with renewable energy sources for reliable charging, even during load-shedding.
  • Automated Billing: Individual RFID access and a central platform ensure usage is tracked and billed accurately, with zero administrative effort.
